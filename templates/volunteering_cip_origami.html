CIP Origami Workshops

Leading origami workshops at CIP has been one of the most meaningful experiences in my volunteer work. What started as teaching a craft has evolved into deep, meaningful connections with the seniors I work with.

Even though I only see them a few times a month for about an hour each session, the relationships we've built are profound. The seniors feel comfortable opening up to me, sharing stories, and treating me like family. There's one senior in particular who I speak Mandarin and Taiwanese with, and she often shares personal stories with me, treating me like her granddaughter.
