Cyber Seniors

I'm proud to have been recognized as the June Volunteer of the Month for my work with Cyber Seniors! This organization helps seniors learn and navigate technology, bridging the digital divide and empowering older adults to connect with their families and communities.

Through one-on-one sessions and group workshops, I help seniors learn how to use smartphones, tablets, computers, and various apps. It's incredibly rewarding to see their confidence grow as they master new skills and discover the possibilities that technology offers.
